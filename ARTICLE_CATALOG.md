# Article Catalog — panggung.id

## Scope

This catalog expands 18 parent topics into 108 neutral Indonesian article briefs about temporary and permanent stages. Articles are planned under `/artikel/<slug>/`. Rental intent stays with `/panggung/`, `/sewa/`, and the existing equipment routes. No brief is a place-name swap, and overlap across other owned domains is allowed.

Catalog statistics: 18 topics; 108 briefs; 12 P0; 79 P1; 17 P2; 0 P3. Every topic has six briefs.

Priority legend: P0 = bounded launch cluster; P1 = essential authority/safety expansion; P2 = specialist or later lifecycle depth; P3 = optional only after stronger evidence. Waves are W1 launch, W2 planning/structure, W3 systems/safety/build, W4 advanced/procurement, and W5 operations/lifecycle.
